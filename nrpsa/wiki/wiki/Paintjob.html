Paintjob

From Nexus RP: Wiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Paintjobs can be applied on a vehicle using setVehiclePaintjob.

To remove a paintjob from a vehicle, apply paintjob number 3 to it.

Supported vehicles

  • Blade: 0,1,2
  • Broadway: 0,1
  • Camper: 0
  • Elegy: 0,1,2
  • Flash: 0,1,2
  • Jester: 0,1,2
  • Remington: 0,1,2
  • Savanna: 0,1,2
  • Slamvan: 0,1,2
  • Sultan: 0,1,2
  • Tornado: 0,1,2
  • Uranus: 0,1,2
