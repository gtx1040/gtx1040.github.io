NRP:San Andreas

Nexus RP itself should be up-to-date with the latest version either by updating in game or by downloading the latest version from the Nexus RP website.

Drivers

It is always wise to install the latest drivers for your hardware, this improves stability and/or performance for both your hardware and software.
